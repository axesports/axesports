    To be a owner of a team & franchise register from the Player Owners only registration link. Player owners however will have to bid to buy a Team Name than buy min 4 to 6 players.

    ━Bidding players pool━
    Players who wants to participate in bidding of APL will have to pay 100 rupees. A player's minimum base value will be kept as 200 rupees for any owner (season I only). The difference in player's base value will go to Ax Esports which will be utilized in Man of the match, Match winning fees later.

    ━Winner━
    An APL Team owner will recieve the match winning fees for every match. And the total money collected will be alloted as first, second, third prize and will be handed over to the owners of the respective teams.
